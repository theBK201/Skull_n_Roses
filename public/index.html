Game Rules

Contents

6 tribes are represented in the game. Each of them owns a collection of sacred object composed of:

  • 3 flower-discs
  • 1 skull-disc
  • 1 card-playing mat assorted to each tribe

Setup

Each player will be assigned randomly with a tribe. You will be able to see your disk with your tribe mat but you won't be able to see other players disks.

Who will be first is also randomly selected.
